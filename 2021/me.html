About me, in a few words

I'm a Drexel graduate, electronic music addict, Vermonter, minimalist, science fiction nerd, and avid Spotify playlist creator. I've been designing professionally for 4+ years, on both large and small teams, with a commitment to collaboration, self-improvement, and communication.

I’ve been able to use my background in graphic design to further push and evolve my skills in UI design, UX design, and front-end development. This influence, and my experiences in print, branding, and packaging, has given me a broad perspective on what it means to be a creative and problem-solver.

I’ve been able to focus my career (so far) on designing platforms, solutions and systems for brands and agencies alike. Much of this work has focused on branding design, design systems, and UI design, but I’ve always been passionate about being informed and involved in every step of the process, from ideation to final build.
